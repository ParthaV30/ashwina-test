Our Story

Built on Expertise,
Driven by Excellence

Aswinaa Fabrication is Chennai's trusted name in glass and aluminium works. Based in K.K. Nagar, we have spent over a decade delivering precision-engineered fabrication solutions to homes, corporate offices, and commercial spaces across Tamil Nadu.

Every project we undertake reflects our commitment to quality materials, skilled craftsmanship, and timely delivery. We treat every space as a canvas — blending structural integrity with visual sophistication.
